CHAC 2024 Scholarships

Deadline for applications :June 30, 2024

We are pleased to announce the availability of Early Career Researchers Scholarships for CHAC 2024. The scholarships are specifically aimed at early career researchers who meet the following criteria:

  • Priority will be given to individuals who have obtained a PhD within the last 8 years or have more than 5 years of experience in climate change and health work contributing to Africa.
  • Sub-Saharan Africa presenters will be given preference.
  • Applicants aged 40 years and below will be considered.

Application Requirements

  • Submit Motivational letter (250 words).
  • Submit updated curriculum vitae.
Please take note of the following terms and conditions:
  • Scholarships are available exclusively for abstract submitters.
  • Applying for the scholarship does not guarantee funding.
  • Full scholarships will be awarded (includes international flights, accommodation, and conferencing).
